# This is the first public alpha release.

You can use and test:

- login to existing Wikimedia account
- Wikimedia account registration
- upload images to Wikimedia Commons and tag them with categories and depict-items
- search WikimediaCommons images (Search-Tab) and browse their tags to discover more images
- explore images and locations on the map
- see all your previous uploads in the Home-Tab
- see all your recently viewed images (that you viewed in the app) in the Home-Tab

Have fun!
